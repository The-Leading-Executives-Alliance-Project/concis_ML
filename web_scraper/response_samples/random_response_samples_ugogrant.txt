Funding is available to support you to engage experiential learning opportunities and create new student-led initiatives.</p><p>Grants are available to partially offset expenses for a variety of experiential learning opportunities, including but not limited to:</p><ul><li>Participating in a volunteer or service learning program</li><li>Competing in a case competition</li><li>Attending a conference</li><li>Organizing a new event, program or service that benefits other BCom students</li></ul></div></div></div></div>

    <div class="fieldable-panels-pane">
